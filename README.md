### 🏢 The SnapSolve Ecosystem
**One Subscription. Zero Friction.**
SnapSolve is a unified suite of tools where design is the primary language. A single subscription carries across the entire family of apps, ensuring a beautiful, fast, and intelligent experience across all workflows.

#### 🦞 Later: The High-Fidelity Productivity Suite
Later is more than a workspace; it’s a living interface. It combines the structural depth of **Notion** with the agility of **TickTick**, redefining productivity through visceral feedback. Every task and transition is met with Duolingo-style charm and Phantom-tier fluidity.

#### 🚇 Relay: The Infinite Team Clipboard
The invisible thread connecting your devices and your team. Relay is a cloud-synced clipboard residing in your system tray, browser, and pocket. Copy code, video, or design assets, and they "relay" to your teammates instantly.

#### 🎚️ SideBy: The Intelligence Engine for Comparison
Eliminating tab-fatigue through AI-driven comparison frameworks. Whether weighing **Rust vs. Go** or **Tokyo vs. Lisbon**, SideBy visualizes data through interactive, animated graphs and side-by-side modules.
